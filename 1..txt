Installation of MetaMask and study spending Ether per transaction
✅ Installation of MetaMask (One-Line Steps)

1️⃣ Go to https://metamask.io
 → click Download → choose your browser → click Add Extension.
2️⃣ Open the MetaMask icon → click Create a Wallet.
3️⃣ Set a strong password → click Create.
4️⃣ Write down the Secret Recovery Phrase → confirm it → click Done.
5️⃣ MetaMask is now installed and ready to use.

✅ Study Spending Ether Per Transaction (One-Line Steps)

1️⃣ Open MetaMask → choose an account.
2️⃣ Click Send → enter receiver address → enter amount of ETH.
3️⃣ Check Gas Fee displayed under "Estimated Gas Fee".
4️⃣ Click Edit (optional) → view Low / Market / Aggressive gas options.
5️⃣ Total ETH spent = Amount Sent + Gas Fee (displayed before confirming).
6️⃣ Click Confirm to send and view Actual Gas Used under Activity → Transaction Details.
7️⃣ On the transaction, click View on Explorer to see Gas Used × Gas Price = Cost in ETH.
